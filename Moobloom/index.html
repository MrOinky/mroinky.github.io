What is a Moobloom?

A Moobloom is a flower-cow hybrid, originating from the game "Minecraft Earth". They are an extremely rare species, with only a 0.5% drop chance from cow tappables in Earth (thats about 1 moobloom in every 200 tappables!). Of course, like their close relative, also hybrid cow, the Mooshroom (mushroom-cow) they like to live in some of the hardest to find places, only appearing in the rare and magical Flower Forests, with scenery that's just as colourful as them. A moobloom contributes to the ecosystem by providing fresh flowers for bees to pollinate with, as well as just being the cutest friend duo you've seen! Mooblooms are a colourful and cheery friend made out of happiness and fun to bring cheer to your world all year round.

Fun Fact:

Mooblooms and bees would be the first cross-mob interaction between two unique passive mobs in Minecraft! (presumably bees follow mooblooms and collect pollen, a potentially very cute sight!)
